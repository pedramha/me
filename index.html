Hello There!

My name is Pedram Hamidehkhan and I'm a Solutions Architect, Programmer, life-time learner and a Coffee Lover!

I come from the City of Tehran, Iran. I did my bachelor studies in Software Engineering in Tehran and worked there for a couple of years. I worked as a Technical Support Staff when I was student, and after finishing my studies started as a Software Engineer. The project that I worked on ranged from Software in Real State business to Big Data and Data Warehousing in Banking.

In 2016 I moved to Germany to pursue my master studies in Computer Science. I had the great opportunity to work as a working student in great places like Bosch and Fraunhofer during my studies.

Currently I live in Stuttgart and work as a Solutions Architect and Backend Engineer for an Automative Company.

I speak English, German, French and Farsi.
